## 5. Research / Technology / Innovation (revised)

ReadyLegacy introduces a new approach to digital legacy management by integrating structured life planning, AI-assisted memory preservation, and post-loss family guidance within a single secure ecosystem.

**Current Technology Stack (MVP):**
- **Frontend**: React 19, TypeScript, Vite — modern, performant single-page application
- **AI Assistant**: RAG-based (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) chat system with multilingual knowledge base covering estate planning, legal frameworks, and bereavement guidance
- **Internationalization**: Custom localization engine supporting 4 languages (English, German, Russian, Ukrainian)
- **Deployment**: Vercel edge network with automatic CI/CD from GitHub
- **Security**: HTTPS, encrypted data handling, permission-based access architecture

**Beta Architecture (in development):**
- Backend API with PostgreSQL database and encrypted document storage
- OAuth 2.0 authentication (Google, Apple, Facebook) + email/password
- Zero-knowledge encryption for sensitive documents
- Role-based access control for trusted contacts (deputies)

**Future AI Capabilities:**
- **AI Legacy Avatars**: Using fine-tuned language models trained on personal writings and voice recordings to create interactive digital representations that future generations can engage with
- **Voice Reconstruction**: Leveraging speech synthesis technology to preserve a person's voice for audio messages, built on existing open-source voice cloning frameworks
- **Intelligent Document Analysis**: AI-powered extraction of key information from uploaded legal documents (wills, insurance policies, property deeds)
- **Smart Suggestions Engine**: Personalized recommendations based on user profile gaps — e.g., "You have a mortgage but no life insurance listed"
- **Immersive Remembrance**: 3D memorial environments using WebGL/WebXR for interactive family heritage exploration

The core innovation lies not in any single technology, but in structuring the entire legacy lifecycle — Prepare, Leave, Remember — within one integrated platform, transforming fragmented personal data into a meaningful and organized digital legacy.

The project is currently in the early development stage. The founding team has self-funded all development to date, including the working MVP prototype, domain registration, and hosting infrastructure.

**Funding Request:**
Venture Kick Stage 1 funding (CHF 10,000) will be allocated as follows:
- **60% — Product Development**: Backend infrastructure, authentication system, encrypted document vault, and beta platform completion
- **20% — User Acquisition & Validation**: Pilot user onboarding, user research interviews, and feedback collection tools
- **10% — Legal & Company Formation**: Swiss GmbH registration, terms of service, privacy policy, and data protection compliance
- **10% — Marketing & Partnerships**: Landing page optimization, initial outreach to B2B partners (funeral homes, estate planners), and content creation

**Business Model:**
- **B2C Subscriptions**: Free tier (basic vault) + Premium at CHF 15/month or CHF 120/year
- **Family Plan**: CHF 25/month for up to 5 family members
- **B2B / White-Label**: Licensed integrations for institutional partners (banks, insurance companies, funeral service providers) — recurring SaaS fees
- **Affiliate Revenue**: Referral commissions from partner services (life insurance, legal advisors)

**Revenue Projection (Switzerland only):**
- Year 1: 500 users, 50 paying → ~CHF 6,000 ARR
- Year 2: 5,000 users, 500 paying + 2 B2B partners → ~CHF 90,000 ARR

**Next Funding Milestones:**

## 10. Development Plan (revised)

Venture Kick funding will support development of the first functional beta version of ReadyLegacy and early market validation.

The development roadmap is structured in clearly defined phases:

**Phase 0 — MVP Foundation (Completed)**
The team has already built a working prototype including a multilingual landing page, interactive estate planning tools (Asset Overview, Will Builder, Legal Framework, Bereavement Path), an AI-powered chat assistant, and demo authentication — deployed and publicly accessible.

**Phase 1 — Backend & Authentication (Q2 2026)**
Real user accounts with secure authentication (Google, Apple, email), encrypted cloud storage for user data, GDPR-compliant data handling, and CI/CD infrastructure.

**Phase 2 — Be Ready Core Product (Q3 2026)**
Full digital document vault, comprehensive asset inventory, jurisdiction-specific will builder (Switzerland first), PDF export, readiness score dashboard, and reminders system.

**Phase 3 — Sharing & Trusted Contacts (Q3–Q4 2026)**
Role-based access for trusted individuals (spouse, lawyer, executor), granular permissions, activation protocols, and activity logging.

**Phase 4 — Leave Behind Module (Q4 2026)**
Video, audio, and written messages for loved ones with scheduled and trigger-based delivery, memory albums, and AI-assisted legacy creation.

**Phase 5 — Be Honored Module (Q1 2027)**
Structured remembrance environment, personalized bereavement pathway, executor dashboard, and memorial pages.

Within the next 3–9 months, the team aims to:
- complete Phase 1 (backend, authentication, encrypted storage)
- launch the beta version with core Be Ready functionality
- onboard 50–100 pilot users in Switzerland
- validate user workflows and iterate based on feedback
- formally establish the company entity
- begin partnership conversations with estate planners and funeral service providers.

## 11. Sustainability (revised)

ReadyLegacy contributes to multiple dimensions of sustainability:

**Reducing paper and administrative waste.** By digitizing estate planning documents, wills, and personal records, the platform eliminates the need for physical filing systems, repeated printing, and redundant paperwork. Families no longer need to reconstruct scattered information from paper archives, reducing both material waste and administrative overhead.

**Lowering the societal cost of unpreparedness.** In Switzerland alone, approximately 72,000 families per year face the administrative and emotional burden of settling a loved one's affairs — often without organized information. ReadyLegacy reduces the time, cost, and emotional toll of these processes by ensuring critical information is structured and accessible in advance.

**Long-term digital preservation.** Unlike physical documents that degrade, get lost, or are destroyed, the platform provides secure, encrypted, long-term storage for personal heritage — ensuring that important records and memories are preserved across generations.

**Supporting mental health and well-being.** The platform's bereavement support module provides structured emotional guidance for grieving families, reducing isolation and connecting people with professional resources — contributing to long-term psychological well-being.

**Enabling responsible digital identity management.** As digital footprints grow, unmanaged accounts and data become environmental and security liabilities. ReadyLegacy helps individuals take responsibility for their digital identity, ensuring conscious management of digital assets rather than abandonment.
