## Phase 1: Backend & Auth — Q2 2026
*Goal: Real user accounts and secure data storage*

- [ ] Backend API (Node.js / .NET) with database (PostgreSQL)
- [ ] Real authentication: Google, Apple, Facebook OAuth + email/password
- [ ] User profiles with settings and preferences
- [ ] Encrypted cloud storage for user data (migrate from localStorage)
- [ ] Session management and JWT tokens
- [ ] GDPR compliance: data export, deletion, consent management
- [ ] SSL + security audit
- [ ] CI/CD pipeline with staging environment

**Deliverable:** Users can register, log in, and their data persists across devices.

## Phase 2: Be Ready (Core Product) — Q3 2026
*Goal: Full estate planning toolkit*

- [ ] **Document Vault**: Upload, organize, and tag legal documents (PDF, images)
- [ ] **Asset Inventory**: Complete financial overview — bank accounts, securities, real estate, crypto, insurance
- [ ] **Will Builder v2**: Guided wizard with jurisdiction-specific rules (Switzerland first)
- [ ] **Power of Attorney & Living Will**: Interactive generators with PDF export
- [ ] **Digital Legacy Manager**: Passwords, accounts, subscriptions, social media directives
- [ ] **Emergency Contacts & Instructions**: Critical info accessible to trusted people
- [ ] **Readiness Score**: Dashboard showing completion percentage across all categories
- [ ] **PDF Export**: Generate formatted documents for printing/notary
- [ ] **Reminders & Notifications**: Annual review prompts, document expiry alerts

**Deliverable:** A user can fully organize their estate plan digitally.

---

## Phase 3: Sharing & Trusted Contacts — Q3-Q4 2026
*Goal: Securely share with family and advisors*

- [ ] **Deputy System**: Invite trusted people (spouse, lawyer, executor) with role-based access
- [ ] **Granular Permissions**: Share specific documents/sections, not everything
- [ ] **Activation Protocol**: Define conditions for when deputies gain full access
- [ ] **Invitation flow**: Email/SMS invites with onboarding for deputies
- [ ] **Activity Log**: Who accessed what, when
- [ ] **Notifications for Deputies**: Alerts when new documents are shared

**Deliverable:** Users can share their plan with trusted people securely.

---

## Phase 4: Leave Behind (Pillar 2) — Q4 2026
*Goal: Create meaningful legacy content*

- [ ] **Message Vault**: Text, audio, and video messages for loved ones
- [ ] **Scheduled Delivery**: Messages delivered on specific dates (birthdays, milestones)
- [ ] **Trigger-based Delivery**: Messages released upon verified life event
- [ ] **Memory Albums**: Photo/video collections with stories and context
- [ ] **Values & Wisdom Journal**: Templates for life lessons, family history, traditions
- [ ] **AI-Assisted Writing**: Help users articulate their thoughts and messages
- [ ] **Voice Cloning / AI Avatar** (experimental): Preserve voice for future messages

**Deliverable:** Users can create a meaningful digital legacy for their loved ones.

---

## Phase 5: Be Honored (Pillar 3) — Q1 2027
*Goal: Support for those left behind*

- [ ] **Bereavement Pathway v2**: Personalized step-by-step guidance after loss
- [ ] **Practical Checklists**: Country-specific post-death procedures (Switzerland, DACH region)
- [ ] **Emotional Support Hub**: Curated resources, grief stages, self-care tools
- [ ] **Memorial Pages**: Digital tribute pages for honoring loved ones
- [ ] **Community Support**: Moderated peer support groups
- [ ] **Professional Directory**: Certified therapists, notaries, estate lawyers
- [ ] **Executor Dashboard**: Task management for estate executors
- [ ] **Message Delivery**: Released messages from the deceased to recipients

**Deliverable:** Families receive structured support through the most difficult time.

## Phase 8: Monetization & Growth — Q2-Q3 2027
*Goal: Sustainable business model*

- [ ] **Freemium Model**:
  - Free: Basic vault (5 documents), readiness score, checklists
  - Premium (~15 CHF/month): Unlimited storage, sharing, AI, video messages, PDF export
  - Family Plan (~25 CHF/month): Up to 5 family members
- [ ] **B2B / Enterprise**: White-label for banks, insurance companies, law firms, funeral homes
- [ ] **Affiliate Partnerships**: Life insurance, legal services, funeral planning
- [ ] **Payment Integration**: Stripe (cards, TWINT for Switzerland)
- [ ] **Subscription Management**: Upgrade, downgrade, cancellation flows

**Deliverable:** Revenue-generating platform with clear value tiers.
